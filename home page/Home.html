About Us

PoultryPalan is a smart digital platform designed to modernize poultry farm management. We help farmers manage their farms, monitor animals, and maintain strong biosecurity practices using simple and efficient tools.

🎯 Our Mission

To empower farmers with digital solutions that improve productivity, reduce disease risks, and ensure sustainable poultry farming.

💡 What We Offer

  • Farm & user management
  • Animal tracking system
  • Biosecurity monitoring
  • Easy and mobile-friendly access

🌱 Why Choose Us

We focus on healthy birds, better farm efficiency, and prevention of diseases through strong biosecurity practices, helping farmers increase their income.
